Musically 2021

Musically is a Cultural and Music Fest Which Is Hosted By The Youth India. It Provides An Opportunity To All The candidates Of Various Colleges/University And Any Other Firm To Compete And Showcase Their Talent In Various Fields Of Club, Cultural and Music Events. The Fest Will Be Organised From December 4 to December 6 2021, Comprising Of Many Type Of Activities Along With several Club Activities. The Fest Comes Up With Many Cultural Activities This Time With Cultural Evenings Which Will Make The Participants Enjoy To The Fullest.
↪ Fully Enthusiastic Environment
↪ More Than 20 Musical And Cultural Events
↪ Participants From Various Colleges Around India

Major Events at GlanceMusically Is A Professionally Managed Event

Cultural Programs

We know that the students who sing and dance well gets very less opportunities to showcase their talents. Cultural events such as dance competetion, singing competetion, fashion shows and AD MAD are organized.

Non-Stop Party

Endless DJ parties, gatherings, bonfire, Celebrity night are waiting for you.

Club Events

Club events are basically indoor events including games and other activities such as open mic, chess championship, scavenger hunt and IPL Auction. Thease events are for fun & entertainment of students of different Universities.
